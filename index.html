Loading a trailer shouldn't be guesswork.

Overloaded axles. Bad tongue weight. Cargo shifting on the highway. Most trailer loading problems happen because there's no easy way to check your setup before you drive. SteadyTow changes that.

Everything you need to load safely

Real-Time Load Analysis

See how every item affects tongue weight, axle loads, and towing capacity as you build your load. Uses moment-balance physics with support for single, tandem, and triple axle trailers.

Visual Load Builder

Drag and drop cargo on a top-down trailer view. See exactly where each item sits and how it affects balance.

Securement Analysis

Know how many tie-downs you need and where. Analyzes braking and lateral forces using FMCSA-based standards.

Hazard Detection

Get warnings for overloaded axles, dangerous tongue weight, hitch mismatches, and under-secured cargo.

VIN Lookup

Enter your VIN and SteadyTow pulls your tow vehicle's ratings directly from the NHTSA database. No manual lookups or guessing required.

Works Offline

All calculations happen on your device. No account required. No data uploaded. Available wherever you are.
